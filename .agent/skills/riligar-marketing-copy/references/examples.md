# Marketing Copy Examples

## Headlines (Contrarian Hook + Specific Promise)

**Bad (Generic)**:

- "The Best AI Tool for Your Business"
- "Transform Your Workflow Today"

**Good (Elevated Direct Response)**:

- "I Deleted 47 Apps and Built a $2M Business with Spreadsheets"
- "Why 'Boring' Marketing Beats Viral Content (My 3-Year Case Study)"
- "The $0 Tool That Replaced My $500/mo Tech Stack"

## Subheadlines (Specifics + Proof + Friction Reducer)

**Bad**:

- "Sign up to learn more"

**Good**:

- "Join 12,847 founders who get the weekly breakdown. Free. No spam. Unsubscribe anytime."
- "The exact 5-step system I used to grow from $0 to $47k MRR. Takes 10 minutes to implement."

## Segment-Specific Messaging

### Job Seekers

**Pain:** Need professional image fast, on a budget
**Message:** "Land your dream job. Look the part."
**Proof:** "Your profile photo affects interview callbacks by 40%"

### Founders/Entrepreneurs

**Pain:** Bootstrap mentality, time-poor, image matters for funding
**Message:** "Look funded. Spend $19."
**Proof:** "First impressions matter. VCs check your LinkedIn before the pitch."

### Sales Professionals

**Pain:** Profile views = pipeline, trust signals matter
**Message:** "More profile views = more pipeline"
**Proof:** "3 new clients in 4 days. One new headshot."

### Real Estate/Financial Advisors

**Pain:** Multiple platforms, trust-based business
**Message:** "One upload. Every platform covered."
**Proof:** "8 different looks for LinkedIn, Zillow, and your website."
